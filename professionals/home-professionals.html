asktoks.com is a platform for parents of children with autism or other challenging behaviours, to seek guidance, treatment and support anonymously. All interactions without our team are strictly confidential.

We believe we can change the world’s response to autism and mental health issues. By sharing the information we have, we are helping to break down stigma and neutralise fear. We do this because changing perceptions is only possible through education and shared information.

More and more professionals who have been trained abroad are now moving back to Nigeria and setting up treatment facilities. Meet aspiring professionals being trained in this country and taking online courses to enter the field of autism.

As the advocacy and awareness programs continue to spread across the nation, more and more parents are acknowledging the spectrum of disorders and addressing their children’s needs positively. The demand for more professionals qualified to cater to the needs of children with developmental disorders is increasing so join the community and grow your network today.

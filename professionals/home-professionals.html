Asktoks.com is a platform for parents of children with autism or challenging behaviours to seek guidance, treatment and support anonymously.

We believe we can change the worldâ€™s response to autism and mental health issues. By sharing the information we have, we are helping to break down stigma and neutralize fear. We do this because changing perceptions is only possible through education and shared information.

We offer individual treatment services, intensive programs, private consultation, corporate services and more. These services benefit children who engage in behaviour that interferes with their development of new skills, restricts opportunities for social interaction and participation in integrated settings or endangers themselves or others. The assessment process involves interviews with the parents and primary caregivers, observation of the child in a familiar environment engaging in familiar routines and observation in a therapeutic setting. During the observation sessions some stimuli and toys may be used to assess various skills.
